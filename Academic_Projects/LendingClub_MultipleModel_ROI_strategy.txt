Predicting loan approval for Lending Club and created a new bucketless ROI strategy

SUMMARY:
Designed an investment strategy to predict if a loan will lead to ‘Charged Off’ or ‘Fully Paid’ on a 7GB training dataset. Experimented with several supervised machine learning models and achieved best f1 score of 82% with random forest
